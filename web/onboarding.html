Step 04

The discovery

These questions surface achievements you may not have on your resume. This is what makes our tailoring exceptional — we find the hidden gold in your experience.

What's the largest team you've led or coached?
Helps us frame your leadership scope for senior roles.
What's the highest-value project or budget you've managed?
Dollar figures are powerful differentiators in resumes.
Name 2-3 measurable improvements you've driven.
Metrics with % or $ beat vague descriptions every time.
What certifications or credentials do you hold?
Certifications are high-signal for ATS filters.
Describe a challenge you overcame that you're proud of.
This becomes a STAR story for cover letters and interview prep.
What tools and technologies are you most proficient with?
Ensures we don't miss technical keywords that ATS systems scan for.
What industries have you worked in?
Cross-industry experience is reframed as adaptability for target sectors.
Is there anything about your experience you feel is underrepresented?
This is often the most valuable answer — skills you use daily but never put on paper.
